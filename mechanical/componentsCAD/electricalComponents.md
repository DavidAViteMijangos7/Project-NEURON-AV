## This folder contains the 3D models used as reference for the CAN project chassis assembly.
* Jetson Orin Nano Dev Kit from NVIDIA Official. Available in [NVIDIA Download Center](https://developer.nvidia.com/embedded/downloads) 
* RPLidar A1 M8	from Slamtec. Available in [Slamtec Support](https://www.slamtec.com/en/Support)
* JGB37-520 Motor	from GrabCAD Community. Available in	[GrabCAD Library](https://grabcad.com/library)

### "All .STEP and .IGS files in this folder are property of their respective manufacturers and are used solely as mechanical references to ensure assembly accuracy and interference detection within SolidWorks."
